Tea Break Pancake

Description

A stack of round pancakes. A staple originating from afternoon tea in manors. Only the best berries should be picked for this dish.

Ingredients

  • 3 Berries
  • 2 Flour Sacks
  • 1 Bird Egg

Steps

  1. Head to the nearest stove or campfire.
  2. Cook the dish!
  3. When your character dies in battle, consume this dish to revive them and restore 400 HP.
