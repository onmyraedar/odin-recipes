Golden Shrimp Balls

Description

A deep-fried shrimp dish. The aroma assaults your senses, while the crispy potatoes bring out the light sweetness of the shrimp meat. This, in tandem with its cute, small shape, makes it very enticing indeed.

Ingredients

  • 5 Shrimp
  • 4 Potatoes
  • 3 Flour Sacks

Steps

  1. Head to the nearest stove or campfire.
  2. Cook the dish!
  3. When your character dies during battle, consume this dish to revive them and restore 1,200 HP.
